Wuhan Municipal Commission of Health and Health on pneumonia of new coronavirus infection
Issuing authority: Wuhan City health committee | Published: 2020-01-15 21:39:12 | Hits:  26835  | Font Size:  Tai Zhong Small
    2020 1 1 Yue 4 Ri 0-24, the city no new novel coronavirus infection in cases of pneumonia, not cured disease cases and no new deaths were reported.

    Cut to the present, the city's cumulative reported cases of pneumonia novel coronavirus infection in 41 cases have been cured and discharged from hospital 7 patients in the intensive treatment of 6 cases of death in 1 case, the rest of the patient's condition stable, patients were treated in isolation in Wuhan City designated medical institutions . A total of 763 close contacts have been tracked , 450 medical observations have been lifted , and 313 medical observations are still underway. Among the close contacts, no related cases were found.
