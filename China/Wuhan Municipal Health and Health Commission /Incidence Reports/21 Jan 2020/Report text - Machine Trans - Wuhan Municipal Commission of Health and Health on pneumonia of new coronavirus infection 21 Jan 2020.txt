Wuhan Municipal Commission of Health and Health on pneumonia of new coronavirus infection
Issuing authority: Wuhan City health committee | Published: 2020-01-21 04:18:02 | Hits:  78594  | Font Size:  Tai Zhong Small
From 12:00 on January 19, 2020 to 14:00 on January 19, 2020, there was one death, no discharged cases and no new cases.

The deceased, Chen, male, 89 years old, developed symptoms on January 13 , 2020, was admitted to the hospital for severe breathing difficulties on January 18, and died on January 19 at 23:39 . Patients have basic diseases such as hypertension, diabetes, coronary heart disease, and frequent ventricular premature beats.

As of 24:00 on January 19, 198 cases of pneumonia of new coronavirus infection have been reported in our city, 25 cases have been cured and 4 cases have been discharged. Currently, 169 patients are still being treated in the hospital, of which 35 are critically ill and 9 are critically ill. They are all under isolation treatment at designated medical institutions in Wuhan.
